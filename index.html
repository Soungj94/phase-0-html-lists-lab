Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich Recipe

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon of jelly
  • Jelly Steps
    • Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
    • Spread jelly on the other slice of bread
    • Put the two slices of bread together
  1. Get bread, peanut butter, and jelly
  2. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  3. Put bread on frying pan and heat until golden brown
  4. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  5. Jelly Steps
    • Spread jelly on the other slice of bread
    • Put the two slices of bread together
